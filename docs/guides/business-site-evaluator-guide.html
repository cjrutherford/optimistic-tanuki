Business Site evaluator guide

Business owner onboarding, hosted tenant sites, and live editor validation

Use this guide when reviewing the multi-tenant business application locally. It summarizes seeded users, where the hosted owner sites are available, and the core owner/client flows that should work end to end.

Primary routes

Platform landing page:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/
Shared auth entry:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/auth
Owner onboarding/editor:
/owner/onboarding and /owner/site-editor
Hosted owner business sites:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/sites/<tenant-slug>

What to evaluate

  1. The platform landing page should explain owner and client capabilities, not render a single tenant site.
  2. Owner sign-in should support onboarding and then route into the editor/workspace.
  3. The live editor should update theme, personality, identity, content, and preview state together.
  4. Hosted tenant routes should show the seeded tenant’s own branding, offers, and messaging.
  5. Client-facing routes should still resolve from the hosted site into booking, contact, and client portal workflows.

Recommended evaluator path

  1. Open / and confirm the platform marketing page is visible, including the owner CTA and the directory of published businesses.
  2. Visit /sites/north-star-advisory and confirm the hosted tenant route shows tenant-specific branding, offers, and client portal entry points.
  3. Sign in at /auth with owner@localbusiness.test and confirm the owner is routed to /owner/dashboard.
  4. Open the site editor from the owner workspace, update theme color, personality, monogram, business title, and at least one content section.
  5. Save the site, open the hosted tenant route again, and confirm the saved owner updates render on the public site.
  6. Use the client credentials listed below to complete the public booking, owner approval, client portal, routine, and billing loop.

Step-by-step testing script

Use this script for manual evaluation. Start with a clean browser context when switching between owner and client accounts so each flow is tested with the expected authentication state.

1. Validate the platform entry point

  1. Open http://127.0.0.1:4303/.
  2. Confirm the page is the platform marketing page, not a single tenant business site.
  3. Verify the page includes owner-facing copy, client-facing copy, and links to the seeded published businesses.
  4. Click Start as an owner and confirm it routes to /auth.
  5. Switch the auth mode to Client and confirm it routes to /client/login.

Expected: the root route explains the platform, exposes both owner and client entry points, and lists the seeded business sites.

2. Validate hosted tenant routing

  1. Open /sites/north-star-advisory.
  2. Confirm the page shows North Star Advisory, tenant-specific hero copy, offers, testimonials, and contact details.
  3. Confirm the primary CTA links to /sites/north-star-advisory/book.
  4. Confirm the secondary CTA links to the tenant-scoped client portal.
  5. Repeat for at least two other seeded tenants, such as /sites/steady-hand-contracting and /sites/ovenbird-bakeshop.

Expected: each tenant route renders distinct branding, offers, copy, and calls to action without requiring owner login.

3. Register a new owner

  1. Open /owner/register.
  2. Fill first name, last name, a unique email, password, password confirmation, and the business-building prompt.
  3. Click Create owner account.
  4. Confirm the new owner is routed to /owner/onboarding.

Expected: owner registration creates the account, signs the owner in, and routes directly into guided onboarding.

4. Complete guided onboarding

  1. In /owner/onboarding, confirm the guided setup rail is visible.
  2. Update the business name, monogram, tagline, short intro, long bio, credentials, and specializations.
  3. Update theme color, personality, card radius, and button style from the theme panel.
  4. Confirm the live preview updates as fields change.
  5. Click Save Changes.
  6. Confirm the success message appears and the owner can navigate to the owner dashboard.

Expected: onboarding creates or updates the site configuration and saves the owner into a published or ready-to-publish state.

5. Test the site editor in studio mode

  1. Sign in as owner@localbusiness.test and open /owner/site.
  2. Switch from Guided Setup to Studio.
  3. Select the hero section and edit the rich content.
  4. Add a custom section, image block, and gallery block.
  5. Confirm each new block appears in the live preview before save.
  6. Reorder sections with drag and drop and confirm the preview order changes.
  7. Save changes.
  8. Open /sites/north-star-advisory and confirm the saved changes render on the public site.

Expected: studio mode supports live preview, rich content editing, section creation, image and gallery editing, reordering, and saved public rendering.

6. Test the owner workspace dashboard

  1. Sign in as owner@localbusiness.test and open /owner/dashboard.
  2. Confirm the dashboard shows prospects, pending bookings, completed bookings, and any enabled routine/check-in metrics.
  3. Navigate through the workspace subnav to Requests, Clients, Availability, Invoices, Checkout, Payments, and Site Editor when those features are enabled.

Expected: the owner workspace keeps operational summaries and navigation scoped to the active business site.

7. Publish availability

  1. Open /owner/availability.
  2. Add a weekly availability slot with day, start time, end time, hourly rate, and service type.
  3. Add a date-specific override in either blocked or available mode.
  4. Confirm the calendar displays weekly availability and overrides.
  5. Edit and remove one availability entry.
  6. Edit and remove one override entry.

Expected: owner availability controls which published time slots are offered to clients, and overrides can block or open specific dates.

8. Submit a public consultation request

  1. Open a private or clean browser context.
  2. Open /sites/north-star-advisory.
  3. Click the primary booking CTA.
  4. Fill name, email, phone, requested offer, available hour block, primary goal, and context.
  5. Click Request consultation.
  6. Confirm the page shows the in-review confirmation for a non-accepted client.

Expected: a public or non-accepted client creates a lead/intake record and is not allowed to book directly as an accepted client.

9. Approve the lead from the owner queue

  1. Return to the owner context and open /owner/requests.
  2. Find the new prospect in the Needs response section.
  3. Click Accept client.
  4. Confirm the prospect moves out of the needs-response section or is marked as accepted.

Expected: owner approval converts the lead into an accepted client who can request bookings against published availability.

10. Register and sign in a client

  1. In a client context, open /sites/north-star-advisory/client/register.
  2. Create an account with first name, last name, email, password, password confirmation, and bio.
  3. Confirm registration signs the client in and opens the tenant-scoped dashboard.
  4. Sign out, then sign in again at /sites/north-star-advisory/client/login.

Expected: client registration links the new account to the hosted site relationship and the client login routes into the client portal.

11. Book as an accepted client

  1. Sign in as client@localbusiness.test or the newly accepted client account.
  2. Open /sites/north-star-advisory/book.
  3. Confirm the page title and submit button say Book session.
  4. Select an offer, published time slot, goal, and context.
  5. Click Book session.
  6. Confirm the booking confirmation appears.

Expected: accepted clients create bookings instead of new intake leads, and the booking is visible in the client dashboard.

12. Approve, complete, and invoice a booking

  1. Sign in as the owner and open /owner/requests.
  2. Find the accepted-client booking in Ready to schedule.
  3. Click Approve booking.
  4. Click Mark complete.
  5. Click Generate invoice.
  6. Confirm the row shows the completed status and generated invoice total.

Expected: the owner queue moves the booking through approved, completed, and invoice-due states.

13. Manage approved clients and routines

  1. Open /owner/clients.
  2. Select an approved client from the roster.
  3. Confirm the client detail shows contact context, bookings, and routine status.
  4. Assign a routine with title and summary.
  5. Confirm the routine appears in the selected client detail and in the shared routines board.

Expected: the owner client studio surfaces approved relationships, bookings, routines, and the next owner action.

14. Use the client portal home and dashboard

  1. Sign in as the accepted client and open /sites/north-star-advisory/client.
  2. Confirm the portal home shows the client-specific next action, dashboard CTA, and booking CTA.
  3. Open /sites/north-star-advisory/client/dashboard.
  4. Confirm the dashboard shows the current business, upcoming sessions, routines when enabled, check-ins when enabled, and other published businesses.

Expected: the client portal is tenant-scoped and reflects the client’s current relationship stage.

15. Complete client routines and check-ins

  1. Open /sites/north-star-advisory/client/routines.
  2. Confirm assigned routines are listed.
  3. If client completion is enabled, mark a routine complete.
  4. Select a routine, fill energy and notes, and submit a check-in.
  5. Confirm the check-in is saved and visible in the client tasks view.

Expected: client routines and check-ins keep the active client relationship moving after booking approval.

16. Review client billing

  1. Open /sites/north-star-advisory/client/billing.
  2. Confirm the billing page lists invoices after a completed session has been invoiced.
  3. Confirm session history includes the approved and completed booking.
  4. If online payment is enabled, click Pay now on an unpaid invoice.

Expected: client billing shows invoices and booking history for completed work.

17. Test the pending-client path

  1. Sign in as pending-client@localbusiness.test.
  2. Open /sites/north-star-advisory/book.
  3. Confirm the page still asks for consultation details instead of direct booking.
  4. Submit a consultation request.
  5. Open the owner requests queue and confirm the request appears as a prospect awaiting approval.

Expected: non-accepted signed-in clients create reviewable leads and cannot bypass owner approval.

18. Test same-login owner access

  1. Use a client account that should also own a business, such as client@localbusiness.test.
  2. Register or sign in through the owner registration path.
  3. Confirm the user can enter the owner onboarding/workspace path with the same login.

Expected: an existing client can claim owner access and reach the owner workspace without needing a separate seeded owner account.

19. Validate the accountant workflow

  1. Sign in as owner-accountant@localbusiness.test and open /owner/dashboard.
  2. Confirm the dashboard surfaces bookkeeping, tax, and payroll service metrics.
  3. Open /owner/availability and add a bookkeeping review slot and a quarterly tax planning slot.
  4. Sign in as client@localbusiness.test and book a bookkeeping review at /sites/ledgerline-accounting/book.
  5. Approve and complete the booking from the owner requests queue, then generate an invoice.
  6. Open the client portal and confirm the client can see upcoming deadlines and bookkeeping context.

Expected: the accountant preset supports advisory services, intake, availability, portal context, and invoicing like other service businesses.

20. Validate the artist commission and storefront workflow

  1. Sign in as owner-artist@localbusiness.test and open /owner/dashboard.
  2. Confirm the owner site uses the Emberline Studio dark theme and commission-focused content.
  3. Open /owner/site and confirm the landing page includes a storefront section and commission booking CTA.
  4. Sign in as client@localbusiness.test and book a portrait commission consult at /sites/emberline-studio/book.
  5. Approve the booking from the owner queue and confirm the owner can track commission details in the client portal.
  6. If store products are enabled, confirm the storefront section renders available work and the client can view the product list.

Expected: the artist preset supports commission bookings, client portal handholding, and visible store inventory without breaking the existing service flow.

Owner flow checklist

  • Platform root renders as marketing content, not a tenant site.
  • Owner registration routes to guided onboarding.
  • Owner login routes to dashboard or onboarding based on completion state.
  • Guided onboarding updates brand, identity, content, and theme.
  • Studio mode supports live preview, section editing, images, galleries, and reordering.
  • Saved site changes render on the hosted tenant route.
  • Owner dashboard summarizes prospects, pending bookings, completed bookings, routines, and check-ins.
  • Requests queue supports accepting prospects, approving bookings, completing bookings, and generating invoices.
  • Clients page supports approved-client roster, booking detail, routine assignment, and shared routine review.
  • Availability page supports weekly slots, date overrides, calendar display, edit, and remove actions.
  • Finance navigation is visible when invoices are enabled.
  • Seeded owner accounts span North Star Advisory, Steady Hand Contracting, Clearcrest Pressure Washing, Ovenbird Bakeshop, Canopy Tree Service, Ledgerline Accounting, and Emberline Studio.
  • Accountant and artist owners can complete intake, scheduling, approval, invoicing, and client portal workflows like other service tenants.
  • The artist storefront preset shows commission booking and store section rendering without breaking the site editor.

Client flow checklist

  • Public tenant site renders unique branding, offers, testimonials, and contact information.
  • Public booking CTA routes to the tenant-scoped booking page.
  • Client registration creates an account and links it to the hosted site.
  • Client login routes to the tenant-scoped dashboard.
  • Non-accepted clients create consultation requests for owner review.
  • Accepted clients can request bookings from published availability.
  • Client portal home shows relationship-specific next action copy.
  • Client dashboard shows current business, sessions, routines, check-ins, and other published businesses.
  • Client routines support assigned work, completion when allowed, and check-in submission.
  • Client billing shows invoices and completed session history when invoices are enabled.
  • All seeded owner types—consulting, contracting, pressure washing, bakery, tree service, accounting, and artist—support the same client booking and approval flows.
