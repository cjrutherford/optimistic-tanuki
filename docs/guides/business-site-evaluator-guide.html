Business Site evaluator guide

Business owner onboarding, hosted tenant sites, and live editor validation

Use this guide when reviewing the multi-tenant business application locally. It summarizes seeded users, where the hosted owner sites are available, and the core owner/client flows that should work end to end.

Primary routes

Platform landing page:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/
Shared auth entry:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/auth
Owner onboarding/editor:
/owner/onboarding and /owner/site-editor
Hosted owner business sites:
http://127.0.0.1:4303/sites/<tenant-slug>

What to evaluate

  1. The platform landing page should explain owner and client capabilities, not render a single tenant site.
  2. Owner sign-in should support onboarding and then route into the editor/workspace.
  3. The live editor should update theme, personality, identity, content, and preview state together.
  4. Hosted tenant routes should show the seeded tenant’s own branding, offers, and messaging.
  5. Client-facing routes should still resolve from the hosted site into booking, contact, and client portal workflows.

Seeded owner accounts and hosted site URLs

After the dev seed runs, these owner accounts are available. Their public business sites are viewable directly at the hosted tenant routes listed below.

  • Successful sign-in should route to the owner workspace and allow access to /owner/site.

Recommended evaluator path

  1. Open / and confirm the platform marketing page is visible.
  2. Visit one hosted site under /sites/<tenant-slug> and confirm the tenant branding is unique.
  3. Sign in at /auth with an owner account and open the site editor.
  4. Change theme color, personality, monogram, and business title; verify the preview and shell update immediately.
  5. Confirm the same tenant route reflects saved owner updates after refresh.
