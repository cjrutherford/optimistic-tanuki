Towne Square is the public-facing name for the `local-hub` product: a local-first platform for neighborhood coordination, commerce, and civic participation.

## Visual Identity

| Attribute           | Value                                                                                 |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Default personality | `soft-touch` — organic, warm, gentle, pill-shaped surfaces                            |
| Why                 | Reinforces a community-feeling product where neighbors meet, trade, and coordinate.   |
| Typography          | Quicksand headings, Nunito body                                                       |
| Borders & shadows   | Pill radius, soft glow                                                                |
| Animation           | Slow, flowing                                                                         |
| Catalog reference   | [`docs/design-system/personalities.md`](../design-system/personalities.md#soft-touch) |

## Audience

- residents who want one place for local information and exchange
- organizers and moderators who need a trusted community surface
- community builders supporting neighborhood-scale participation
- operators who want a deployable local platform instead of disconnected community tools

## Promise

Bring neighborhood commerce, communication, and participation into one local platform that feels useful day to day, not only during major events.

## Workflow

1. Launch a city, neighborhood, or community surface.
2. Invite residents and organizers into the same local context.
3. Support classifieds, direct messages, chat, donations, sponsorships, and seller workflows.
4. Give operators a platform that can grow from coordination into local commerce.

## Proof

- city and community browsing flows
- classifieds for local buying, selling, and offers
- direct messaging and community chat
- donation, sponsorship, and seller-wallet workflows
- operator and deployment docs for the app and its supporting services
- local-hub e2e coverage in the workspace

## Deployment Posture

Towne Square runs as the `local-hub` Angular SSR app in the shared repo stack. It participates in the same Docker, Nx, and generated deployment workflows as the rest of the platform.

## Call To Action

Start with one city or neighborhood, launch the shared local surface, and invite residents to post, coordinate, and support the community in one place.
