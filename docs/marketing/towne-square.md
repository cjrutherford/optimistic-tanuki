# Towne Square

Towne Square is the public-facing name for the `local-hub` product: a local-first platform for neighborhood coordination, commerce, and civic participation.

## Audience

- residents who want one place for local information and exchange
- organizers and moderators who need a trusted community surface
- community builders supporting neighborhood-scale participation

## Promise

Bring neighborhood commerce, communication, and participation into one local platform that feels useful day to day, not only during major events.

## Proof

- city and community browsing flows
- classifieds for local buying, selling, and offers
- direct messaging and community chat
- donation, sponsorship, and seller-wallet workflows
- operator and deployment docs for the app and its supporting services

## Call to Action

Start with one city or neighborhood, launch the shared local surface, and invite residents to post, coordinate, and support the community in one place.
